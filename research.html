DIA and SIMCE tests socioemotional research

This third research I worked on in my fourth year with a group of classmates. We had to analyze the socioemotional section of the DIA and SIMCE test results. Once we had the information analyzed we had to develop an action plan to improve one variable we discovered was low at the school and that we thought was crucial for students’ proper socioemotional development. The variable we worked on was: “Es fácil para mí decirle a los demás qué emociones estoy sintiendo (por ejemplo, le digo a los demás que estoy sintiendo pena, rabia, alegría, miedo, etc.)”.
