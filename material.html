Material

In this section of the portfolio, you will have available some activities and materials for math, literacy and class council that I have been doing for my lessons at school during these five years at college. The idea is that you can take ideas from them or even use them in your own classrooms. All the activities are very concrete and most of them use concrete material. But why concrete material? I think using concrete material is the best way of learning for students. Learning by having fun and by experiencing real things help students internalize the content better and look at it in a closer way.
