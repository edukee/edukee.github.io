UDL

Chart UDL

The chart shows the different things the teacher can do in their planning or classroom to make the lessons friendlier for all kind of student’s necessities. I have been using this chart by implementing some of the points on my plannings during all fifth year of college. I invite you to use the chart because with these simple things you can make your classes approachable and achievable for all your students whether they have diagnosed learning difficulties or not. We must understand that all students have difficulties in different degrees, so all of them will be winning with these simple implementations and adaptations.

Project Minor Discapacidad e Inclusión

I did this project with some classmates in the minor of disability and inclusion at Universidad de Los Andes. The objective of this project is to train and teach teachers at schools about the different NEE and how we can handle them inside and outside the classroom. During each training session the teachers will have to analyze real cases and look for a solution, so the learning will be meaningful and real.
